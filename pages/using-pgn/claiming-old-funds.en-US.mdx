# Claiming tokens

Search for your address in the [funds-proofs.txt](/funds-proofs.txt) file. Addresses are checksummed so you can use a tool like [Ethereum Address Checksum](https://ethsum.netlify.app/) to checksum your address.

If you find your address in the file, you are eligible to claim your tokens by calling the `claim` function of the BalanceClaimer contract. 


**Additionally**, you can use the `canClaim` read function to check if your proof is valid without actually claiming the tokens.

## Steps to claim funds with Etherscan

<Steps>
### Step 1

**(1)** Download and open the [funds-proofs.txt](/funds-proofs.txt) file for later use
    - You can quickly search your address to check if you should follow the steps below to claim funds or not.
    - If you can't find it, you don't have funds to claim.

**(2)** Open the [Etherscan's **BalanceClaimerProxy** page](https://etherscan.io/address/0x0ca4c7a370e0155c77a33e78443a54d749e0bc21#code).

### Step 2

![Step 2](/claim-steps/steps-2.jpg)

**(3)** Make sure you're on the Contract/code tab, if not, click on the Contract button in Etherscan.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  Make sure the contract address is **0x0Ca4C7A370E0155c77a33e78443a54D749E0BC21**
</Callout>

### Step 3

![Step 3](/claim-steps/steps-3.jpg)


**(4)** Click on the [Read as Proxy](https://etherscan.io/address/0x0ca4c7a370e0155c77a33e78443a54d749e0bc21#readProxyContract) button

**(5)** Connect your wallet to Etherscan.


### Step 4

![Step 4](/claim-steps/steps-4.jpg)

**(6)** Click on the [canClaim](https://etherscan.io/address/0x0ca4c7a370e0155c77a33e78443a54d749e0bc21#readProxyContract#F4) function, this will allow you to enter your details and check before trying to claim funds

### Data copy

#### Copy your claim data as highlighted below for next steps

![Step 5](/claim-steps/steps-txt-highlight.jpg)

### Step 5

![Step 5](/claim-steps/steps-5.jpg)

<Callout type="info" emoji="ℹ️">
  While this step is optional, we still recommend you follow through to make sure all data is copied correctly and will work when claiming your funds.
</Callout>

**(7)** Find your address in [funds-proofs.txt](/funds-proofs.txt) and fill in each input with your information.
    - These are color coded in the image above to help with visual mapping
    - Make sure to copy the data correctly.

**(8)** Click the Query button to execute the `canClaim` function with your data
    - It will return either `true` or `false`

### Step 6

![Step 6](/claim-steps/steps-6.jpg)


**(9)** If you *can* claim funds (`true` response from previous step), you can now click on the [Write as Proxy](https://etherscan.io/address/0x0ca4c7a370e0155c77a33e78443a54d749e0bc21#writeProxyContract) button

**(10)** Reconnect your wallet on this page

### Step 7

![Step 7](/claim-steps/steps-7.jpg)


**(11)** Click on the [claim](https://etherscan.io/address/0x0ca4c7a370e0155c77a33e78443a54d749e0bc21#writeProxyContract#F1) function and fill in the same information from the previous `canClaim` function. Proof, address, ethBalance and erc20Balance.

**(12)** Click the `Write` button.
    - This will trigger a transaction with your wallet to claim your funds
    - Check the tx information and if everything looks good, confirm your transaction.

### Step 8

<Callout type="info" emoji="✅">
    Done! Once the transaction is confirmed, you should have your funds available in your address as soon as the transactions is processed.
</Callout>
</Steps>
